## Stock Visualization Summary

This document contains visualizations of Apple's stock price data over the last 6 months.

### 1. Price Trend (Line Chart)

This chart displays the closing price of Apple stock over time. It provides an overview of the general price trend during the period.

### 2. Candlestick Chart

This chart provides a more detailed view of the price action, showing the open, high, low, and close prices for each day. The color of the candlestick indicates whether the closing price was higher (green) or lower (red) than the opening price.

### 3. Volume Chart

This chart shows the trading volume for each day. It can be used to identify periods of high or low trading activity, which may be associated with significant price movements.

### 4. Moving Averages

This chart displays the 20-day and 50-day moving averages of Apple's stock price. Moving averages can help smooth out the price data and identify trends. Crossovers between the moving averages can be used as potential buy or sell signals.
